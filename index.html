About Us

Patel Enterprise is a micro-enterprise engaged in trading activities, specializing in the wholesale and retail sale of motor vehicles and motorcycles. Our business was established with the goal of providing quality products and exceptional service to our customers.

We pride ourselves on our commitment to excellence and our dedication to meeting the needs of our clients. Our enterprise operates from Gandhinagar, Gujarat, and has been serving the community since its incorporation in 2024.
